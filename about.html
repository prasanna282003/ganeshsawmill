Our Humble Beginnings

Our Story

Founded in 1978 by Mr. Rajesh Kumar, Sri Ganesh Sawmill began as a small family-operated workshop with just two employees. What started as a modest operation processing local timber has grown into one of the region's most respected wood suppliers.

Our founder's vision was simple: provide quality wood products while maintaining ethical business practices and sustainable sourcing. This philosophy remains at the core of everything we do today, three decades later.

In 2005, we expanded our operations with modern machinery while preserving traditional wood seasoning techniques. This combination of innovation and tradition sets us apart in the industry.
